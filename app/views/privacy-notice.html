Privacy notice

Who we are

This service is run by the Department for Environment, Food and Rural Affairs (DEFRA).

Why we collect your personal data

We collect your personal data to process your wind turbine grant application. This includes:

  • your name and contact details
  • your business information
  • details about your property
  • information about your proposed wind turbine installation

How we use your data

We use your personal data to:

  • process your grant application
  • contact you about your application
  • arrange installation of your wind turbine
  • process grant payments
  • comply with legal and regulatory requirements

Who we share your data with

We may share your data with:

  • installation contractors
  • other government departments as required by law
  • our data processors who help us run this service

How long we keep your data

We keep your personal data for 7 years after your application is closed, in accordance with government record keeping requirements.

Your rights

Under data protection law, you have the right to:

  • request access to your personal data
  • request correction of inaccurate data
  • request deletion of your data
  • restrict how we use your data
  • request your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact data@defra.gov.uk

Contact us

For more information about how we use your data, contact our Data Protection Officer:

DEFRA Data Protection Officer
Email: dataprotection@defra.gov.uk
