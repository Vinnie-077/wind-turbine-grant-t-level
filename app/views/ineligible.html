Why you are not eligible

Based on the information you provided, you do not meet the eligibility criteria for the wind turbine grant.

This may be because:

  • you do not own the business
  • the business does not own the property or land
  • your property is not connected to the National Grid
  • you do not have enough available space (minimum 200 square feet required)
  • you have not agreed to the 20-year turbine placement commitment

What to do next

  • check you have entered the correct information and start a new application
  • contact your local authority for advice on alternative grants
  • visit the Green Deal energy saving measures page for other options
  • speak to a renewable energy advisor about your options
