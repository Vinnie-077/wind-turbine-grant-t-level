Use of this service

This service is provided by DEFRA to allow you to apply for wind turbine grants. By using this service, you agree to these terms and conditions.

Accuracy of information

You must ensure that all information you provide in your application is accurate and complete. Providing false or misleading information may result in:

  • your application being rejected
  • recovery of any grant payments made
  • legal action

Grant eligibility

By submitting an application, you confirm that you meet all eligibility criteria for the wind turbine grant. These criteria include:

  • owning your business
  • owning the property or land where the turbine will be installed
  • your property being connected to the National Grid
  • having sufficient available space for installation
  • agreeing to keep the turbine in place for at least 20 years

Grant conditions

If your application is approved and you receive a grant:

  • you must keep the turbine in place for at least 20 years from installation
  • you must maintain the turbine in safe working condition
  • you must allow inspection by DEFRA representatives
  • any changes to the installation require prior approval
